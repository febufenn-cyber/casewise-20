# Phase 5 exit review

Status: repository implementation complete after this increment. Confidential production use, independent security verification, legal-domain evaluation and practitioner workflow approval remain pending.

## Implemented

- source-bound narrative support sets and sentence objects;
- attribution, dispute, uncertainty, omission and materiality preservation;
- unsupported-language and prohibited-conclusion blockers;
- immutable reviewed matter-overview snapshots;
- deterministic source manifests and exact source reopening identifiers;
- allegation-row response-planning snapshots;
- factual-answer, evidence-verification, client-question, contradiction-resolution and authority-research task nodes;
- active-matter assignments and acyclic dependencies;
- exact-version attorney approval for overview and plan snapshots;
- source and structured-object dependency graphs;
- stale invalidation of overviews, plans, approvals and internal exports;
- internal-only, non-filing-ready export manifests;
- observation-level Phase 5 evaluation;
- citation-fidelity, unsupported-language, support-coverage, correction-severity, omission, stale-propagation and review-time gates;
- RLS, audit events, tests, inventory validation and CI integration.

## External gates still open

1. Independently deploy and verify the Phase 1 authentication, RLS, isolation, deletion, queue, malware, provider and incident controls.
2. Obtain permissible design-partner matter packs and independently annotate expected overview sentences, plan nodes and source links.
3. Demonstrate zero unsupported material factual statements and zero material omissions.
4. Demonstrate that every material sentence and plan node opens the correct exact source.
5. Exercise upstream corrections and verify complete stale propagation in deployed infrastructure.
6. Calibrate support-coverage, correction-severity and time-savings thresholds by matter type.
7. Demonstrate meaningful lawyer review-time savings without increased omission or correction severity.
8. Obtain litigation and arbitration practitioner approval for overview and planning terminology and workflow.
9. Confirm provider, retention, privacy, regional and commercial terms before confidential model-assisted use.

## Decision

The Phase 5 repository layer is ready to support provider-neutral fixtures and controlled evaluation. It is not authorized for confidential production use and is not evidence that Casewise produces legally correct matter overviews or response plans.

Phase 6 may implement a provider-neutral authority-research architecture under the handoff contract. Live authority providers and production research remain blocked until source licences, provider terms, credentials, retention rules and jurisdiction metadata requirements are approved.
