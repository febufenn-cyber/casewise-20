# Immediate Founder Evidence Actions

These are the only Phase 0 tasks that cannot be completed through repository design alone.

## Week 1

- Identify 15 candidate lawyers or legal teams matching the initial ICP.
- Book at least five workflow interviews.
- Ask every participant for a sanitized current work-product example.
- Score every candidate using the design-partner scorecard.
- Do not pitch final drafting; test the matter-map job.

## Week 2

- Secure at least two permissible matter packs.
- Produce manual or paper-prototype outputs for one pack.
- Run a source-verification session with a user.
- Record accepted, edited, rejected, and omitted items.
- Measure baseline and review time.

## Commercial action

Propose a 30–45 day paid pilot to the strongest candidate with explicit page limits, exclusions, data terms, review sessions, success metrics, and stop conditions.

## Decision action

Update the assumption register, decision log, risk register, and decision gates with evidence. Record one explicit outcome: proceed, pivot, pause, or kill.
