# Critical facts contract

Dates retain their source text, precision, certainty, possible interpretations, and semantic type. A month-only date is represented as a range and never silently promoted to a stated day.

Amounts retain raw text, currency, normalized value, Indian scale, and conflict warnings. A pleading's amount is an asserted amount, not an admitted fact.

Document references are first-class objects. A reference to an annexure does not establish that the annexure was uploaded; resolution may remain ambiguous or referenced-but-absent.
