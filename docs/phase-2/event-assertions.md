# Event assertion contract

Phase 2 stores source claims about events, not established facts. Affirmations, denials, qualified responses, reported statements, quotations, inferences, and uncertain extractions remain distinct assertion modes.

Every assertion requires one or more source spans. Repeated wording from copied pleadings may be marked as duplicate without being counted as independent corroboration. A court or tribunal document that quotes a party remains a quoted assertion unless the document separately makes a finding.
