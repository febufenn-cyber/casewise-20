# Product Research

This directory stores research instruments and non-confidential evidence used to validate Casewise.

## Rules

- Do not commit confidential client facts, privileged communications, identifying matter details, or raw customer files.
- Prefer coded participant identifiers in interview notes.
- Store only the minimum evidence needed to support a product decision.
- Obtain permission before recording direct quotations.
- Keep matter packs under `evals/` only after provenance and permitted use are documented.
- A design partner is qualified by behavior—workflow artifacts, permissible data, repeated review, or a payment path—not by compliments.

## Current artifacts

- `interview-guide.md` — structured workflow, trust, data, and commercial interview.
- `design-partner-scorecard.csv` — comparable candidate qualification.

Interview notes are intentionally not pre-created in the public repository. Use an approved private research system until a safe repository policy is selected.
